# Details on 3D printing

- I am using the JLC3DP print service to produce this, which has a cost of about $10 + shipping.
- The models should have roughly these proportions:
15×6.25×4.2 cm
- Access the source files at onshape: https://cad.onshape.com/documents/c1f604bc7dcb19d0034320b9/w/e582f7a5ec8be5c9c5e2ed68/e/27ea5781da26a9e15f82bfe8?renderMode=0&uiState=69e3839507cea94ea421fe85
- They are intended to be painted after printing, so consider this when choosing the colour of material to use.
