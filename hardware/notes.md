The voltage regulator needs to be able to deal with the level of current drawn by the circuit. So, the standard AMS1117-3.3 is insufficient.

# Scale
## Dimensions of PCB:
57 mm height
75 mm width
(with tolerances)
## Dimensions of display
27.8mm height
23.5mm width
(this will fit cleanly within the scale of the PCB)

Hence the dimensions of the opening in the front of the train, for simplicity, should be around the dimensions of the PCB, except wider by about 10mm. 
This isn't exactly precision manufacturing and the display doesn't have a flexible/ribbon connector so it's not a good idea to shrink the size of the opening on the side of the train.
